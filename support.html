Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is creating a Leapa account free?

  • How long until I get my transfer?

  • What kind of cards are supported?

  • I don't have a website, what do I do?

Yes. Leapa account is totally free, We only get a small percentage of fees from your business's transactions.

Leapa transfers are lightningly fast. Our appplication is built on technology focused on performance and speed. The transfers between accounts are instant and fast.

Leapa enables businesses of all sizes to accept payments from all major cards from all over the world. Among them are: Mastercard, Visa, Amex, Diners cards.

You can still use Leapa without a website with our product pages. You can create a Leapa account and create your product's page with necessary information and pricing and share the page with your potential customers. All this like if you had a website.
With Leapa you can still accept payments with no upfront investment in website development.
